WONDER'S OF THE WORLD

There are various marvels created by god and man. These natural and man made edifices are truly an example of beauty and archeological excellence. A lot of research work, study and analysis are done before announcing these structures as Seven Wonders of the World and truly requires a lot of effort that goes into it. The list of new Seven Wonders of the World was released on 7th July of 2007 in Lisbon, Portugal. New and old entries were contending for the title of Seven Wonders of the World. Well the selection was a difficult task indeed. The public from all over the world voted for their favorites. Out of a list of 25 finalist monuments only 7 of them were chosen as the wonders of the world.

The 7 Wonders of the World are:-

  • The Taj Mahal (India)

  • The Chichen Itza (Mexico)

  • The Christ the redeemer (Rio de Janeiro, Brazil)

  • The Great Wall of China

  • The Machu Picchu (Peru)

  • The Petra (Jordan)

  • The Roman Coliseum (Italy)
